Fire Behavior Fuel Model SH7 (147) -- Very High Load, Dry Climate Shrub

Scott and Burgan (2005) classify Fire Behavior Fuel Model SH7 (147) in the Shrub Fuel Type and describe it as follows:

The primary carrier of fire in SH7 is woody shrubs and shrub litter. Very heavy shrub load, depth 4 to 6 feet (1.2 to 1.8 meters). Spread rate lower than SH5, but flame length similar. Spread rate is high; flame length very high.

Fuel model parameters are available by viewing the Fuel Model Table of Parameters or right-clicking on the fuel model description in the Input Guide window and selecting View parameters.

Scott and Burgan's (2005) photographs on page 47 are examples of fuels fitting this model.
