Flat Terrain Spotting Distance from Active Crown Fire

The flat terrain spotting distance from an active crown fire is the maximum spotting distance over flat terrain. This value is then adjusted according to the terrain descriptors. This is an intermediate value in the calculations that might be of interest to someone examining the active crown fire spotting distance model.
