Fire Behavior Fuel Model TL1 (181) -- Low Load Compact Conifer Litter

Scott and Burgan (2005) classify Fire Behavior Fuel Model TL1 (181) in the Timber Litter Fuel Type and describe it as follows:

The primary carrier of fire in TL1 is compact forest litter. Light to moderate load, fuels 1 to 2 inches (2.5 to 5 cm) deep. May be used to represent a recently burned forest. Spread rate is very low; flame length very low.

Scott and Burgan's (2005) photographs on page 57 are examples of fuels fitting this model.
