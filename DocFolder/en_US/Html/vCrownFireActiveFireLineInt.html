Active Crown Fireline Intensity

Active crown fireline intensity assumes the crown fire is fully active. It is calculated from surface fire heat per unit area, crown fire heat per unit area, and active crown fire rate of spread as described by Rothermel (1991). Active crown fireline intensity is used to calculate active crown fire flame length.

Active crown fire outputs are the same for both the Rothermel (1991) and Scott and Reinhardt (2001) crown fire methods.

I/O Module If Notes
Input None    
Output CROWN
