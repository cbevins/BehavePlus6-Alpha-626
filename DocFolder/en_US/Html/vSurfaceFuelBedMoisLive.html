Characteristic Live Fuel Moisture

Characteristic live fuel moisture is the calculated live fuel moisture for the entire surface fuel bed. The calculation involves weighting the fuel moisture by live fuel loading and surface area-to-volume ratio for each live class.

I/O Module If Notes
Input None    
Output SURFACE   Intermediate value
