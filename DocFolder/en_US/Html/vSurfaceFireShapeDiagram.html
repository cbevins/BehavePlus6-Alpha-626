Fire Shape Diagram

Fire shape diagrams of the elliptical shape of a point source surface fire assumes no suppression action and are produced for each result in the table output. Surface fire rate of spread of the head fire and elapsed time give the surface fire heading spread distance. The surface fire direction of maximum spread is determined by the combination of the wind and slope directions. Surface fire length-to-width ratio is a function of effective wind speed.

I/O Module If Notes
Input None    
Output SIZE   A diagram is produced for each result in the table output

BehavePlus (as well as the old BEHAVE and FARSITE) uses a simple ellipse model to characterize fire size and shape. (Anderson (1983) used double ellipse-based equations.) The fire's shape is dependent only on the effective wind speed.
