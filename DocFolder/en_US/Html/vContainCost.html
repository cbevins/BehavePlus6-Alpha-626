Cost of Resources Used

Cost of resources used is the total cost of all resources dispatched to the fire, including both the resource base cost and its resource hourly cost for the time spent on the fire until the fire is contained or its resource duration expires. If multiple resources are dispatched to the fire, and some of those resources are not used for containment, their cost is not included in the cost of resources used (i.e., travel costs are not considered). Units for cost are not assigned; costs can be calculated as, for example, euros, dollars, or hundreds of dollars.
