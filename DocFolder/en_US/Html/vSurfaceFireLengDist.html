Surface Fire Length

Surface fire length is the sum of the surface fire heading spread distance and the surface fire backing spread distance. It is the total distance from the head to the rear of the surface fire and is calculated for an elliptically shaped point source fire.

I/O Module If Notes
Input None    
Output SIZE   Can also be output as a map distance if Display output distances in map units is checked.
