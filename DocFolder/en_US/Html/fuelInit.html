Fuel Parameter Initialization

Custom fuel models are often developed by modifying an existing fuel model. Select the existing fuel model by clicking on (or highlighting) one of the fuel models from the list and press Ok to initialize all fuel parameter inputs with the parameters from the selected model. Fuel models must be attached to BehavePlus before they will show up in this list.
