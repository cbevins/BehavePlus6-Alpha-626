Fire Behavior Fuel Model SB4 (204) -- High Load Blowdown

Scott and Burgan (2005) classify Fire Behavior Fuel Model SB4 (204) in the Slash-Blowdown Fuel Type and describe it as follows:

The primary carrier of fire in SB4 is heavy blowdown fuel. Blowdown is total, fuel bed not compacted, most foliage and fine fuel still attached to blowdown. Spread rate very high; flame length very high.

Scott and Burgan's (2005) photographs on page 70 are examples of fuels fitting this model.
