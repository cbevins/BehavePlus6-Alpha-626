Models Used in BehavePlus

All of the models included in BehavePlus are listed here, separated by module. Citations are included in the list with full references at the bottom of the page. This list has been adapted from Andrews (2014), which was current through version 5. Additional models and modifications incorporated in version 6 are also provided in this table.

CROWN Critical surface intensity needed for transition from surface to crown fire (Van Wagner 1977)
Transition to crown fire, relationship of surface fire intensity and critical surface fire intensity (Van Wagner 1989; Van Wagner 1993; Finney 1998; Scott and Reinhardt 2001)

Active crown fire condition, relationship of crown fire rate of spread and critical crown fire rate of spread (Van Wagner 1989; Van Wagner 1993; Finney 1998; Scott and Reinhardt 2001)
Fire type: surface, torching, conditional crown, crowning (Van Wagner 1993; Finney 1998; Scott and Reinhardt 2001)

Crown fire intensity (Rothermel 1991)
SIZE Elliptical fire size and shape, area, perimeter, length-to-width ratio (Anderson 1983; Andrews 1986)
CONTAIN Fire containment (Albini and others 1978; Fried and Fried 1996; Trethewey in prep.)
SPOT Spotting distance from torching trees (Albini 1979; Chase 1981)
Spotting distance from active crown fire (Albini and others 2012)
Spotting distance from a burning pile (Albini 1981)

SCORCH Crown scorch height (Van Wagner 1973)
MORTALITY Tree mortality (Ryan and Reinhardt 1988; Ryan and Amman 1994; Reinhardt and Crookston 2003; Wang and others 2007; Hood and others 2007)
