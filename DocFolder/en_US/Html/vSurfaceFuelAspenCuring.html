Aspen Curing Level

Aspen curing level is the percent (or fraction) of herbaceous biomass that is dead. This value is used only for the special case aspen fuel models (Brown and Simmerman 1986). It is used to calculate 1-h fuel load, live herbaceous fuel load, live woody fuel load, 1-h SA/V, and live woody SA/V. Aspen curing level is different from the fuel load transfer portion used to represent curing for dynamic fuel models (standard or custom) (Scott and Burgan 2005).
