Fire Behavior Fuel Model TU2 (162) -- Moderate Load, Humid Climate Timber-Shrub

Scott and Burgan (2005) classify Fire Behavior Fuel Model TU2 (162) in the Timber-Understory Fuel Type and describe it as follows:

The primary carrier of fire in TU2 is moderate litter load with shrub component. High extinction moisture. Spread rate is moderate; flame length low.

Scott and Burgan's (2005) photographs on page 52 are examples of fuels fitting this model.
