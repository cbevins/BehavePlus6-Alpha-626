SURFACE & SPOT Module Spread Direction Conflict

Spotting distance from a wind-driven surface fire is estimated for maximum surface fire rate of spread (at the head of the fire). As a result, in the SURFACE Module you must select "Surface fire spread direction is HEADING only (in the direction of maximum spread)" in the Module Options... window when SURFACE and SPOT are used together.

There are two ways to resolve this conflict:

  • In the SURFACE Option... > Input Options > Directions tab, change the surface fire spread option so it is for HEADING only (in the direction of maximum spread), or
  • De-activate the SPOT Module.

Press Cancel to cancel the run and return to the input worksheet.
