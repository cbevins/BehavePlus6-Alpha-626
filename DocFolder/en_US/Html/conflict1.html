SURFACE & SPOT Module Spread Direction Conflict

Because spotting distance from a wind-driven surface fire is estimated from the head of the fire, it may be linked to the SURFACE Module only when "Surface fire spread direction is HEADING only (in the direction of maximum spread)" is selected.

There are two ways to resolve a conflict:

  • Change the surface fire spread direction option so it is for HEADING only (in the direction of maximum spread), or
  • De-activate the SPOT Module.

Press Cancel to cancel the run and return to the input worksheet.
