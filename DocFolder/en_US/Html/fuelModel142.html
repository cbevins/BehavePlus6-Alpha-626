Fire Behavior Fuel Model SH2 (142) -- Moderate Load, Dry Climate Shrub

Scott and Burgan (2005) classify Fire Behavior Fuel Model SH2 (142) in the Shrub Fuel Type and describe it as follows:

The primary carrier of fire in SH2 is woody shrubs and shrub litter. Moderate fuel load (higher than SH1), depth about 1 foot (0.3 meters), no grass fuel present. Spread rate is low; flame length low.

Fuel model parameters are available by viewing the Fuel Model Table of Parameters or right-clicking on the fuel model description in the Input Guide window and selecting View parameters.

Scott and Burgan's (2005) photographs on page 42 are examples of fuels fitting this model.
