Wind Factor

Wind factor is an intermediate value in Rothermel's surface fire spread model (1972) which accounts for the effect of wind on fire spread. The surface fire rate of spread is calculated for zero wind and zero slope (R0) and then increased by the wind factor (phi-w) and the slope factor (phi-s).

  • ROS = R0 × (1 + phi-w + phi-s), where R0 = heat source / heat sink

I/O Module If Notes
Input None    
Output SURFACE   Intermediate value
