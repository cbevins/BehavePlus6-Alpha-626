Fuel Model Table of Parameters

Fuel model parameters, listed in the table below, vary for each model and include: fuel load, fuel model type, surface area-to-volume ratio (SA/V), fuel bed depth, moisture of extinction, and fuel particle heat content. Fuel model parameters not listed are constant for the entire set and include the following:

  • 10-hr SA/V = 109 1/ft
  • 100-hr SA/V ratio = 30 1/ft
  • Total fuel particle mineral content = 5.55 percent
  • Effective (silica-free) mineral content = 1.00 percent
  • Oven-dry fuel particle density = 32 lb/ft3
Metric equivalents for each fuel model can be found in two ways:
  1. Open the example worksheet entitled "FuelModelingMetric.bpw" and select "Initialize from a Fuel Model."
  2. Click on the Guide button next to Fuel Model on any worksheet containing the SURFACE module, right-click on a fuel model, and select "View parameters." Both English and metric units are contained in this file.
