Fire Behavior Fuel Model TL4 (184) -- Small Downed Logs

Scott and Burgan (2005) classify Fire Behavior Fuel Model TL4 (184) in the Timber Litter Fuel Type and describe it as follows:

The primary carrier of fire in TL4 is moderate load of fine litter and coarse fuels. Includes small diameter downed logs. Spread rate is low; flame length low.

Scott and Burgan's (2005) photographs on page 60 are examples of fuels fitting this model.
