SURFACE & SPOT or CROWN Module Wind Conflict

Because the SPOT and CROWN Modules require wind speed at either 20 feet or 10 meters, you cannot enter a midflame wind speed for the SURFACE Module.

There are two ways to resolve a conflict:

  • Configure the SURFACE Module to use either 20-foot or 10-meter wind speed with wind adjustment factor either input or calculated, or
  • De-activate the SPOT or CROWN Module (in which case the run will continue modeling only surface fire behavior).
