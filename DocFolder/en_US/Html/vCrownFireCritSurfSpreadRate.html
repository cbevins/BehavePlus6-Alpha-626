Critical Surface Fire ROS, Crown Fire Initiation

Critical surface fire rate of spread for crown fire initiation is the rate of spread of the surface fire that is required to transition from a surface fire to a crown fire when using the Scott and Reinhardt (2001) method. It is calculated from foliar moisture content, canopy base height, and surface fire heat per unit area.

I/O Module If Notes
Input None    
Output CROWN If Crown fire is calculated using Scott and Reinhardt (2001) is selected as an input option. Intermediate output
