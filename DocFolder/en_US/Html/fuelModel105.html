Fire Behavior Fuel Model GR5 (105) -- Low Load, Humid Climate Grass (Dynamic)

Scott and Burgan (2005) classify Fire Behavior Fuel Model GR5 (105) in the Grass Fuel Type and describe it as follows:

The primary carrier of fire in GR5 is humid-climate grass. Load is greater than GR3 but depth is lower, about 1 to 2 feet (0.3 to 0.6 meters).

Scott and Burgan's (2005) photographs on page 30 are examples of fuels fitting this model.
