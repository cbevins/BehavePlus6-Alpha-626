Critical Crown ROS

The critical crown fire rate of spread (ROS) is the rate at which a crown fire must spread to maintain itself as an active crown fire (Van Wagner 1977). It is calculated from the canopy bulk density. Crown fire rate of spread is compared to critical crown fire rate of spread to determine whether an active crown fire is possible.
