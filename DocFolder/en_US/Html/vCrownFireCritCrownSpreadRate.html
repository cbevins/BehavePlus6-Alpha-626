Critical Crown Fire Rate of Spread

Critical crown fire rate of spread is the rate at which a crown fire must spread to maintain itself as an active crown fire (Van Wagner 1977). It is calculated from canopy bulk density. Crown fire rate of spread is compared to critical crown fire rate of spread to determine whether an active crown fire is possible.
