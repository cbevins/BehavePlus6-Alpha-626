Units Converter Tool

This dialog converts a quantity between arbitrary units of measure.

Controls

  • From Amount
    Enter a single quantity to be converted.
    You may enter an integer or a real value.
    The real value may be in decimal or scientific notation.

  • From Units
    Enter the input units of measure.
    You may use common abbreviations.
    The following are all equivalent and acceptable:
    • pounds per foot2
    • lb/ft2
    • lb / ft2
    • lb ft-2
    • lb per ft2


  • Into Units
    Enter the output units of measure.
    You may use common abbreviations.
    The following are all equivalent and acceptable:
    • kilograms per meter2
    • kg/m2
    • kg / m2
    • kg m-2
    • kg per m2


  • Convert Button
    Press this button to attempt the conversion.
    If successful, the answer appears in the field to its right.
    If unsuccessful, an error message appears describing the problem.
    Errors can result from unknown unit names or abbreviations or from an attempt to convert between incompatible units (such as from feet to kilograms).
