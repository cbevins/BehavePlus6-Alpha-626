Chaparral Days Since May 1st

The number of days since May 1st may be used to estimate the seasnally-dynamic live leaf and live stem heat of combustion. It may also be used to estimate the chaparral seasonally-dynamic live leaf and live stem moisture content.

Dates preceeding May 1st are treated as if they are May 1st. Days after October 31st are treated as if they are October 31st.
