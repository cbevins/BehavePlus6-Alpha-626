Fire Behavior Fuel Model SB1 (201) -- Low Load Activity Fuel

Scott and Burgan (2005) classify Fire Behavior Fuel Model SB1 (201) in the Slash-Blowdown Fuel Type and describe it as follows:

The primary carrier of fire in SB1 is light dead and down activity fuel. Fine fuel load is 10 to 20 t/ac (22 to 45 tonne/ha), weighted toward fuels in the 1 to 3 inch (2.5 to 7.6 cm) diameter class, depth is less than 1 foot (0.3 meters). Spread rate is moderate; flame length low.

Fuel model parameters are available by viewing the Fuel Model Table of Parameters or right-clicking on the fuel model description in the Input Guide window and selecting View parameters.
