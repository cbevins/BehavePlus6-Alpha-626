Effective Wind Speed

Effective wind speed is the combined effect of midflame wind speed and slope steepness in the direction of maximum spread (head fire). This effective wind speed is used to determine the shape (length-to-width ratio) of a point source fire. Calculation of effective wind speed is based on the wind and slope factors that are part of Rothermel's (1972) surface fire spread model.

I/O Module If Notes
Input SIZE If SURFACE is not also selected. If both SURFACE and SIZE are selected, the effective wind speed value for the direction of maximum spread calculated in SURFACE is used in SIZE.
Output SURFACE
