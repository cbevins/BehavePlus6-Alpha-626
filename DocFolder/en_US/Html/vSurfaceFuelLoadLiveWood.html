Live Woody Fuel Load

Live woody fuel load is the weight of that class of fuel per unit area. Live woody fuel load is a fuel model parameter. Live woody fuels include the foliage and very fine stems of living shrubs.

Live woody fuels are perennial and frequently have surface area-to-volume ratios from 1,000 to 2,000 ft2 / ft3 (3,281 to 6,562 m2 / m3).
