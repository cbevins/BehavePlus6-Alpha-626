Aspect

Aspect is the direction a slope faces. When the direction of rate of spread is calculated in terms of North (rather than upslope), aspect is used to determine the direction of upslope. This direction is needed to calculate the combined effect of wind and slope (effective wind speed) on fire spread.

I/O Module If Notes
Input SURFACE If Wind and spread directions are degrees clockwise from north (direction from which the wind is blowing) is selected as an input option  
Output None
