Fire Behavior Fuel Model SH8 (148) -- High Load, Humid Climate Shrub

Scott and Burgan (2005) classify Fire Behavior Fuel Model SH8 (148) in the Shrub Fuel Type and describe it as follows:

The primary carrier of fire in SH8 is woody shrubs and shrub litter. Dense shrubs, little or no herbaceous fuel, fuel bed depth about 3 feet (0.9 meters). Spread rate is high; flame length high.

Scott and Burgan's (2005) photographs on page 48 are examples of fuels fitting this model.
