Fire Behavior Fuel Model SB3 (203) -- High Load Activity Fuel or Moderate Load Blowdown

Scott and Burgan (2005) classify Fire Behavior Fuel Model SB3 (203) in the Slash-Blowdown Fuel Type and describe it as follows:

The primary carrier of fire in SB3 is heavy dead and down activity fuel or moderate blowdown. Fine fuel load is 7 to 12 t/ac (16 to 27 tonne/ha) , weighted toward 0 to 0.25 inch (0 to 0.64 cm) diameter class, depth is more than 1 foot (0.3 meters). Blowdown is moderate, trees compacted to near the ground. Spread rate is high; flame length high.
