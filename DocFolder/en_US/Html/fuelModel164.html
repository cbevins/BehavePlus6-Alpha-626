Fire Behavior Fuel Model TU4 (164) -- Dwarf Conifer With Understory

Scott and Burgan (2005) classify Fire Behavior Fuel Model TU4 (164) in the Timber-Understory Fuel Type and describe it as follows:

The primary carrier of fire in TU4 is short conifer trees with grass or moss understory. Spread rate is moderate; flame length moderate.

Fuel model parameters are available by viewing the Fuel Model Table of Parameters or right-clicking on the fuel model description in the Input Guide window and selecting View parameters.

Scott and Burgan's (2005) photographs on page 54 are examples of fuels fitting this model.
