Active Crown Fire Perimeter

Active crown fire perimeter assumes an elliptically shaped fire. The crown fire is assumed to be active, and the perimeter is calculated from the active crown fire spread distance, 20-ft wind speed, and crown fire length-to-width ratio (Rothermel 1991; p. 16, eqn. 13). Backing fire spread is ignored in this calculation.

Active crown fire outputs are the same for both the Rothermel (1991) and Scott and Reinhardt (2001) crown fire methods.

Input None    
Output CROWN
