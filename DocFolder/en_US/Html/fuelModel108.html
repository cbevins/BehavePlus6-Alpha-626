Fire Behavior Fuel Model GR8 (108) -- High Load, Very Coarse, Humid Climate Grass (Dynamic)

Scott and Burgan (2005) classify Fire Behavior Fuel Model GR8 (108) in the Grass Fuel Type and describe it as follows:

The primary carrier of fire in GR8 is continuous, very coarse, humid-climate grass. Load and depth are greater than GR6. Spread rate and flame length can be extreme if grass is fully cured.

Fuel model parameters are available by viewing the Fuel Model Table of Parameters or right-clicking on the fuel model description in the Input Guide window and selecting View parameters.

Scott and Burgan's (2005) photographs on page 33 are examples of fuels fitting this model.
