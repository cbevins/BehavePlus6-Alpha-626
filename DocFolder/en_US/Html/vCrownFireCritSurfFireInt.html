Critical Surface Fireline Intensity

Critical surface fireline intensity is the fireline intensity of the surface fire that is required for a transition from a surface fire to a crown fire. It is calculated from foliar moisture content and canopy base height.

I/O Module If Notes
Input None    
Output CROWN   Intermediate value
