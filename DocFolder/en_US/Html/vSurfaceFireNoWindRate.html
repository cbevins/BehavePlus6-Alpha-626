Surface Fire No Wind-No Slope Spread Rate

This value appears if you are calcuating passive crown fire behavior using the Scott and Reinhardt (2001) crown fire model without the SURFACE module. Accurate estimates of passive crown fire activity require that you run the SURFACE and CROWN modules together in this situation. It is not possible to accurately estimate the values needed to calculate passive crown fire activity from field measurements.

Surface fire no wind-no slope spread rate is the "speed" at which the fire travels through the surface fuels with no wind and no slope. Rothermel's (1972) surface fire spread model multiplies this value by the slope factor and wind factor to obtain the surface fire rate of spread.
