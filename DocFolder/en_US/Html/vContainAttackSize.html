Fire Area at Initial Attack

Fire area at initial attack is the predicted fire size when the first resource begins suppression action on the fire. It is calculated from the fire size (area) and shape (length-to-width ratio) at report, maximum surface fire rate of spread, and resource arrival time. If the SURFACE, SIZE and CONTAIN modules are run together, the fire area at initial attack will be calculated by the SIZE module.
