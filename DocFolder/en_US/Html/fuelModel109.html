Fire Behavior Fuel Model GR9 (109) -- Very High Load, Humid Climate Grass (Dynamic)

Scott and Burgan (2005) classify Fire Behavior Fuel Model GR9 (109) in the Grass Fuel Type and describe it as follows:

The primary carrier of fire in GR9 is dense, tall, humid-climate grass. Load and depth are greater than GR8, about 6 feet tall (1.8 meters). Spread rate and flame length can be extreme if grass is fully or mostly cured.

Fuel model parameters are available by viewing the Fuel Model Table of Parameters or right-clicking on the fuel model description in the Input Guide window and selecting View parameters.

Scott and Burgan's (2005) photographs on page 34 are examples of fuels fitting this model.
