Flame Height above Canopy of Active Crown Fire

Flame height above canopy of an active crown fire is the average length of flames extending above the top of the canopy. It is used to calculate the height to which a firebrand is lofted. This is an intermediate value in the calculations useful for examining the model for spotting distance from active crown fire.
