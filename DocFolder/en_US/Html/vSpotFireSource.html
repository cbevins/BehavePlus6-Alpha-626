Spotting Source Location

The spotting source location refers to the origin of the firebrands that can result in spotting when there is terrain. Four possible locations are provided.

  • Ridge top (RT)
  • Midslope, windward side (MW)
  • Valley bottom (VB)
  • Midslope, leeward side (ML)
