Sun-Moon Calendar Contents

This page defines the contents of the Sun-Moon Calendar.

Controls

Seasonal Dates
Moon Phases		 Check this box to produce
  • a list of the first date for each season as well as
  • a table of new and full moon dates and times
for the entire year selected in the Location tab.
Sun-Moon Chart Check this box to produce a table of daily sun and moon events for the specified month and year.

Content options include:
  • Sunrise: Check this box to have sunrise times appear in the Sun-Moon chart.
  • Sunset: Check this box to have sunset times appear in the Sun-Moon chart.
  • Moonrise: Check this box to have moonrise times appear in the Sun-Moon chart.
  • Moonset: Check this box to have moonset times appear in the Sun-Moon chart.
  • Civil Dawn: Check this box to have civil dawn times appear in the Sun-Moon chart.
  • Civil Dusk: Check this box to have civil dusk times appear in the Sun-Moon chart.
Sun-Moon Calendar Check this box to produce a calendar for the specified month and year.

Content options include:
  • Sun Times: Check this box to have sunrise and sunset times appear in each day on the calendar.
  • Moon Times: Check this box to have moonrise and moonset times appear in each day on the calendar.
  • Moon Phase: Check this box to mark the calendar with the days of the new moon, the full moon, and the first and third quarters.
