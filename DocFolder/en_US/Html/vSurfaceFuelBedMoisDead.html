Characteristic Dead Fuel Moisture

Characteristic dead fuel moisture is the calculated dead fuel moisture for the whole surface fuel bed. The calculation involves weighting the fuel moisture by dead fuel loading and surface area-to-volume ratio for each dead class.

I/O Module If Notes
Input None    
Output SURFACE   Intermediate value
