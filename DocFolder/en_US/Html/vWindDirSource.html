Wind Direction (from North)

Wind direction measured in degrees from north is the direction from which the wind is blowing. This is the usual way of specifying wind direction. This requires additional information on aspect to determine the direction of upslope in order to consider the combined effect of wind and slope on fire spread.

Alternatively the user can select the option to specify direction of the wind vector, the direction the wind is pushing the fire. It is obviously critical that the user be certain of the method of specification of wind direction.
