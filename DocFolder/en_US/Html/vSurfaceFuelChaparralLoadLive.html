Chaparral Live Fuel Load

The Rothermel and Philpot (1973) dynamic chaparral fuel model uses the dead fuel fraction to split the total chaparral fuel load into dead and live fuel portions.

The total live fuel load is then partitioned as follows where w is the total chaparral fuel load and f is the dead fuel fraction:
