Trainee Name and Date

Trainee name and date identify who is creating an exercise. This is one of the lines added when Training worksheet header is selected.

Other lines can be added to the worksheet header by selecting Configure > Appearance preferences > Worksheet and checking the Prescribed fire plan worksheet header and Fire behavior projection worksheet header check boxes.

Additional description can be added in the Notes section of the worksheet.
