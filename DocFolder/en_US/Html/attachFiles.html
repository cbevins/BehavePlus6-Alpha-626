Attach Fuel Model or Moisture Scenario Files

Folders (and files) with a paper clip icon are currently attached to (e.g., accessible for use by) the BehavePlus application. Individual files are attached by attaching the folder in which they reside. It is not possible to select and attach individual files; the entire folder must be attached. Once a folder is attached, the fuel models or moisture scenarios appear in the appropriate Input Guide window and may be used in the BehavePlus application.

Highlight the folder(s) containing the files you want to attach. Then, press Ok. Once the Ok button is pressed, only highlighted folders will have their files attached. Folders that are not highlighted will NOT have their files attached, even if they are currently marked as attached by the paper clip icons.

Click on a folder to toggle the highlighting. Click on a folder's "+" or "-" to view its files.
