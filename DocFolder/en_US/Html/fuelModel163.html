Fire Behavior Fuel Model TU3 (163) -- Moderate Load, Humid Climate Timber-Grass-Shrub (Dynamic)

Scott and Burgan (2005) classify Fire Behavior Fuel Model TU3 (163) in the Timber-Understory Fuel Type and describe it as follows:

The primary carrier of fire in TU3 is moderate forest litter with grass and shrub components. Extinction moisture is high. Spread rate is high; flame length moderate.

Scott and Burgan's (2005) photographs on page 53 are examples of fuels fitting this model.
