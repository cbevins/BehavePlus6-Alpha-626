Area per Heavy Equipment

The area per heavy equipment is the average area needed for each piece of equipment to be located within the safety zone. For general planning when the equipment types are not known, use a mean area of 300 ft2 (28 m2).

Space is allocated in the safety zone for equipment in case it is brought to the safety zone or is already there for other reasons. However, equipment is a liability in a safety zone; they often carry flammable liquids. In no other situation is heavy equipment allowed to operate in such close proximity to personnel. Heavy equipment or engines should not be brought to a safety zone if it increases travel time to the safety zone or compromises the safety of personnel in the safety zone.
