Passive Crown Fire Area

Crown fire area assumes an elliptically shaped fire. The crown fire is assumed to be passive (torching), and the area is calculated from the passive crown fire spread distance and the 20-ft wind speed (adapted from Rothermel 1991; p. 16, eqn. 12). The calculation also considers the crown fire length-to-width ratio. Backing fire spread is ignored in this calculation.

Passive crown fire outputs are only valid for the Scott and Reinhardt (2001) crown fire method.

I/O Module If Notes
Input None    
Output CROWN If Crown fire is calculated using Scott and Reinhardt (2001) is selected as an input option.
