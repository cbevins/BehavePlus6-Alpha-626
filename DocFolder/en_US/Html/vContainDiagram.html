Containment Diagram

The containment diagram is a diagram of the final fire shape, size, and constructed fireline at the time it was successfully contained or escaped initial attack (all resources are applied and their durations expire before containment is achieved).

I/O Module If Notes
Input None    
Output CONTAIN    

The following containment diagram shows a successful indirect (offset > 0) attack starting at the head of the fire.

The following containment diagram shows a direct attack (offset = 0) from rear that did not contain the fire during the resource's duration.
