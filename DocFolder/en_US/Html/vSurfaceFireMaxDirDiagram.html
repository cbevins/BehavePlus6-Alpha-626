Wind / Slope / Spread Direction Diagram

Wind / Slope / Spread Direction Diagrams are diagrams of the relative directions of slope, wind, and fire spread. They illustrate the relative effect of wind and slope on head fire direction. The arrows indicate directions, not values, for wind, slope, or spread. The diagram heading indicates the selected spread direction option: heading, flanking, backing, from ignition point, or from fire front.

For more information on fire spread direction options, see Fire Spread Directions.
