Wind / Slope / Spread Direction Diagram

Wind / Slope / Spread Direction Diagrams are diagrams of the relative directions of slope, wind, and surface fire spread. They illustrate the relative effect of wind and slope on head fire direction. The arrows indicate directions, not values, for wind, slope, or spread. The diagram heading indicates the selected spread direction option: heading, flanking, backing, from ignition point, or from fire perimeter.

For more information on fire spread direction options, see Fire Spread Directions.

I/O Module If Notes
Input None    
Output SURFACE   The option Display table results must be selected.
