Passive Crown Fireline Intensity

Passive crown fireline intensity assumes the crown fire is passive (torching). It is calculated from surface fire heat per unit area, passive crown fire heat per unit area, and passive crown fire rate of spread as described by Scott and Reinhardt (2001). Passive crown fireline intensity is used to calculate passive crown fire flame length.

Passive crown fire outputs are only valid for the Scott and Reinhardt (2001) crown fire method.

I/O Module If Notes
Input None    
Output CROWN If Crown fire is calculated using Scott and Reinhardt (2001) is selected as an input option.
