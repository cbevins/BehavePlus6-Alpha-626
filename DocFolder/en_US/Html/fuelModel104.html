Fire Behavior Fuel Model GR4 (104) -- Moderate Load, Dry Climate Grass (Dynamic)

Scott and Burgan (2005) classify Fire Behavior Fuel Model GR4 (104) in the Grass Fuel Type and describe it as follows:

The primary carrier of fire in GR4 is continuous, dry-climate grass. Load and depth are greater than GR2; fuel bed depth is about 2 feet (0.6 meters).

Fuel model parameters are available by viewing the Fuel Model Table of Parameters or right-clicking on the fuel model description in the Input Guide window and selecting View parameters.

Scott and Burgan's (2005) photographs on page 29 are examples of fuels fitting this model.
