Active Crown Fire Heat per Unit Area

Active crown fire heat per unit area is the sum of surface fire heat per unit area and canopy heat per unit area. It is used to calculate active crown fireline intensity.

Active crown fire outputs are the same for both the Rothermel (1991) and Scott and Reinhardt (2001) crown fire methods.

I/O Module If Notes
Input None    
Output CROWN
