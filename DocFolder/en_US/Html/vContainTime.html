Time from Report

The time from report is the elapsed time from when the fire was reported until it was either successfully contained, or it escaped the initial attack by the dispatched resources.

I/O

Module

If

Notes

Input

None

 

  

Output

CONTAIN

 

 

A fire has escaped initial attack when all specified resources have arrived at the fire and their duration times have expired without enclosing the fire with fireline.
