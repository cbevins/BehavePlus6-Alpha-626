Fire Behavior Fuel Model TL8 (188) -- Long-Needle Litter

Scott and Burgan (2005) classify Fire Behavior Fuel Model TL8 (188) in the Timber Litter Fuel Type and describe it as follows:

The primary carrier of fire in TL8 is moderate load long-needle pine litter, may include small amount of herbaceous load. Spread rate is moderate; flame length low.

Scott and Burgan's (2005) photographs on page 64 are examples of fuels fitting this model.
