Fire Behavior Fuel Model SH1 (141) -- Low Load Dry Climate Shrub (Dynamic)

Scott and Burgan (2005) classify Fire Behavior Fuel Model SH1 (141) in the Shrub Fuel Type and describe it as follows:

The primary carrier of fire in SH1 is woody shrubs and shrub litter. Low shrub fuel load, fuel bed depth about 1 foot (0.3 meters); some grass may be present. Spread rate is very low; flame length very low.

Scott and Burgan's (2005) photographs on page 41 are examples of fuels fitting this model.
