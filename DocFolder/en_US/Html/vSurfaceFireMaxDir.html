Surface Fire Direction of Maximum Spread

The surface fire direction of maximum spread (head fire) is based on the influence of slope and wind and their relative directions. When the wind is not blowing directly uphill, the calculated direction of maximum spread will be between the uphill direction and the direction in which the wind is blowing (direction of the wind vector). The direction of maximum spread can be expressed as either degrees from upslope or as degrees from north, depending on which input optiont is selected.

I/O Module If Notes
Input None    
Output SURFACE
