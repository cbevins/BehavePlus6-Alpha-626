Fire Behavior Fuel Model TL5 (185) -- High Load Conifer Litter

Scott and Burgan (2005) classify Fire Behavior Fuel Model TL5 (185) in the Timber Litter Fuel Type and describe it as follows:

The primary carrier of fire in TL5 is high load conifer litter; light slash or mortality fuel. Spread rate is low; flame length low.

Scott and Burgan's (2005) photographs on page 61 are examples of fuels fitting this model.
