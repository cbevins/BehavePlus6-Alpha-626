Fire Behavior Fuel Model SH6 (146) -- Low Load, Humid Climate Shrub

Scott and Burgan (2005) classify Fire Behavior Fuel Model SH6 (146) in the Shrub Fuel Type and describe it as follows:

The primary carrier of fire in SH6 is woody shrubs and shrub litter. Dense shrubs, little or no herbaceous fuel, fuel bed depth about 2 feet (0.6 meters). Spread rate is high; flame length high.

Fuel model parameters are available by viewing the Fuel Model Table of Parameters or right-clicking on the fuel model description in the Input Guide window and selecting View parameters.

Scott and Burgan's (2005) photographs on page 46 are examples of fuels fitting this model.
