Geographic Place Browser

Select a geographic place and click Ok. If your area of interest is not listed, click Cancel and enter the latitude and longitude directly.

You can sort any column by clicking on its header. A second click sorts the column in reverse order. Drag the bar between the list and the Help pane to see more information.

It is often helpful to click on (sort by) the County column to review available place names within the state, province, or territory. Then, you can select from the named geographic places.
