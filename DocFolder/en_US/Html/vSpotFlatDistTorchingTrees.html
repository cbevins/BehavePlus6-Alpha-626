Flat Terrain Spotting Distance from Torching Trees

The flat terrain spotting distance from torching trees is the maximum spotting distance over flat terrain. This value is then adjusted according to the terrain descriptors. This is an intermediate value in the calculations that might be of interest to someone examining the spotting distance model.
