Fire Behavior Fuel Model GR6 (106) -- Moderate Load, Humid Climate Grass (Dynamic)

Scott and Burgan (2005) classify Fire Behavior Fuel Model GR6 (106) in the Grass Fuel Type and describe it as follows:

The primary carrier of fire in GR6 is continuous humid-climate grass. Load is greater than GR5 but depth is about the same. Grass is less coarse than GR5.

Scott and Burgan's (2005) photographs on page 31 are examples of fuels fitting this model.
