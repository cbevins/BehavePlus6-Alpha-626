Surface Reaction Intensity

Surface reaction intensity is the rate of the energy release per area (square foot or square meter) within the flaming front. Surface reaction intensity is not affected by wind, slope, or direction of spread in a surface fire.

Surface reaction intensity is part of the Rothermel (1972) surface fire spread model.

I/O Module If Notes
Input None    
Output SURFACE
