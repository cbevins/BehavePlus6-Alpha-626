Fire Perimeter at Initial Attack

Fire perimeter at initial attack is the predicted perimeter of the elliptically shaped fire when the first resource begins suppression action. It is calculated from the fire size (area) and shape (length-to-width ratio) at report, maximum surface fire rate of spread, and resource arrival time. If the SURFACE, SIZE and CONTAIN modules are run together, the fire perimeter at initial attack will be calculated by the SIZE module.
