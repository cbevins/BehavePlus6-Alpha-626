Fire Behavior Fuel Model TL9 (189) -- Very High Load Broadleaf Litter

Scott and Burgan (2005) classify Fire Behavior Fuel Model TL9 (189) in the Timber Litter Fuel Type and describe it as follows:

The primary carrier of fire in TL9 is very high load, fluffy broadleaf litter. TL9 can also be used to represent heavy needle-drape. Spread rate is moderate; flame length moderate.

Scott and Burgan's (2005) photograph on page 65 is an example of fuels fitting this model.
