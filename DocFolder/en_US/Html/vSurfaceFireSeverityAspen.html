Aspen Fire Severity

Aspen fire severity is used for predicting mortality of aspen (Brown and Simmerman 1986, Brown and DeByle 1987).

  • Low fire severity in this case refers to fires that char but do not completely consume leaf litter, where there are patches of unburned vegetation and litter.
  • Moderate+ severity fires consume the litter and some duff.

I/O Module If Notes
Input SURFACE If Fuel is entered as special case fuel model, western aspen is selected as an input option. Required for Probability of Aspen Mortality.
Output None
