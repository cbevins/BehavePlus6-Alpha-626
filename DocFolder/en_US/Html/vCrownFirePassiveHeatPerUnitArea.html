Passive Crown Fire Heat per Unit Area

Passive crown fire heat per unit area is the sum of surface fire heat per unit area and canopy heat per unit area. Identical to the active crown fire heat per unit area, it is used to calculate passive crown fireline intensity.

Passive crown fire outputs are only valid for the Scott and Reinhardt (2001) crown fire method.

I/O Module If Notes
Input None    
Output CROWN If Crown fire is calculated using Scott and Reinhardt (2001) is selected as an input option.
