Downwind Canopy Cover

Downwind canopy cover is the percent of the ground covered by overstory canopy downwind of a spot fire source (in the direction firebrands are carried). It is used to determine if the canopy downwind is Open or Closed. If downwind canopy cover is Open, the downwind canopy height will be divided in half before calculating spotting distance from torching trees.
