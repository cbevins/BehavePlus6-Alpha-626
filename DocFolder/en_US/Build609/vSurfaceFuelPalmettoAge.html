P-G Age of Rough

Palmetto-gallberry age of rough (understory vegetation) is applicable only for the special case palmetto-gallberry fuel model. Age of rough is used in determining the load of all the fuel classes. The age of understory rough is usually the number of years since the last fire; it is an important factor in the calculation of fuel load.

I/O

Module

If

Notes
Input SURFACE If Fuel is entered as special case fuel model, palmetto-gallberry is selected.  
Output None
