Probability of Aspen Mortality

Probability of mortality is the likelihood that a tree will be killed by a fire. Probability of aspen mortality is based on flame length, D.B.H., and fire severity of either low or moderate+ (Brown and Debyle 1987). This calculation is done as part of the special case aspen fuel model that can be selected in SURFACE. It is not included as part of the MORTALITY module.

Probability of aspen mortality can be interpreted as the probability of an individual tree dying or it can be multiplied by the number of trees on a site to estimate the number of trees that will die.
