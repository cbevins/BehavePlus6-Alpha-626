Fire Shape Diagram

Fire shape diagrams of the elliptical shape of a point source surface fire with no suppression action are produced for each result in the table output. Surface rate of spread and elapsed time determine the forward spread distance. Effective wind speed determines the length-to-width ratio.

A diagram is produced for each result in the table output

BehavePlus (as well as the old BEHAVE and FARSITE) uses a simple ellipse model to characterize fire size and shape (Anderson (1983) used double ellipse-based equations). The fire's shape is dependent only on the effective wind speed.
