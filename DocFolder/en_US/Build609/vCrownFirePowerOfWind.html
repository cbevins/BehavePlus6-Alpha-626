Power of the Wind

Power of the wind is described by Rothermel (1991) based on work by Byram (1959). Power of the wind is the rate of kinetic energy in the wind field, and is expressed in the same units as power of the fire. If the power of the wind is greater than the power of the fire, then the fire-wind system is dominated by the energy of the wind. Such fires are wind-driven. Rothermel (1991) developed a method of estimating the spread rate of a wind-driven crown fire.
