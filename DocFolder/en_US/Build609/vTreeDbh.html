D.B.H.

D.B.H. is the abbreviation for diameter at breast high. It is the measurement of the diameter of a tree measured at about 4.5 feet (1.4 meters) from the ground level. It is used to calculate bark thickness in MORTALITY and is part of the torching tree description in SPOT. D.B.H. is also an input for the western aspen special case fuel model.
