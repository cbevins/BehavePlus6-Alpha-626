Length-to-Width Ratio

The length-to-width ratio of an elliptically shaped fire is sometimes called length-to-breadth ratio. The length-to-width ratio is a function of the effective wind speed. Fire burning under a high wind speed will be long and narrow with a large length-to-width ratio. This variable applies to surface fire. Crown fire length-to-width ratio can also be calculated.
