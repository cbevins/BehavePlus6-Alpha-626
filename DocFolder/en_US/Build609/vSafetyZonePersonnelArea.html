Area per Person

The area of a circle is the smallest area that provides the required separation distance. A non-circular safety zone that provides the required protection on all sides will have a greater area than calculated here. Existing or natural safety zones (such as rockslides, barren areas, and some recently burned areas) are rarely circular. For example, the existing elliptical safety zone shown below has a much greater area than the required Circular Safety Zone Size, but does not provide the required separation along the sides.
