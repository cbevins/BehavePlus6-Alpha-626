Area or Fire Size at Report

The area of a fire started that from a point source is based on the elliptical fire shape model. To avoid confusion, the variable is called area when it is an output of the SIZE module. It is called fire size at report when it is an input to the CONTAIN module. Calculation of area is based on maximum rate of spread, effective wind speed, and elapsed time. This variable applies to surface fire. Crown fire area can also be calculated.

BehavePlus, the old BEHAVE, and FARSITE use a simple ellipse model to characterize fire size and shape. The fire's shape is dependent on the effective wind speed. (Anderson (1983) developed double ellipse-based equations to describe fire shape).
