Total Live Fuel Load

Total live fuel load is the sum of the load of all live fuel classes. For standard and custom fuel models this includes live woody and live herbaceous fuel load. The calculation is done after the load transfer is done for dynamic fuel models. This variable is most meaningful in an examination of dynamic fuel models.
