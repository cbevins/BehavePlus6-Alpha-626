Active Ratio

The active ratio is crown fire rate of spread divided by the critical crown fire rate of spread. If the active ratio is greater than or equal to one, then the fire may be an active crown fire. Viewing active ratio provides more information than the Yes or No for the active crown fire variable. It is instructive to know whether the active ratio is close to one or significantly higher than or lower than one.
