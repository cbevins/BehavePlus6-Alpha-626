SURFACE Slope Input Options

This page controls the SURFACE Module's slope units of measure and whether slope is entered directly into the input worksheet or calculated from map measurements.

Options

Slope is entered as....
  percent.

Slope is specified as percent which is 100 times the slope rise divided by the slope reach (or 100 times the tangent of the slope in degrees). A 100% slope has a 45-degree incline.

This selection may be overridden by a custom units set.

degrees. Slope is entered as degrees.
 
Slope steepness is....
  specified on the worksheet. Slope stepness is entered directly.
  calcuated from map measurements. Slope steepness is calculated from a map representative fraction, contour interval, map distance, and number of contours crossed.
