WAF Calculation

WAF (Wind Adjustment Factor) Calculation indicates the method of calculating wind adjustment factor (WAF). If the crown fill portion is less than 5%, the unsheltered model is used; otherwise the sheltered model is used. If WAF is input rather than calculated, the variable is not meaningful and is set to Input.

The variable WAF Calculation can have three values:

  • Sheltered,
  • Unsheltered, or
  • Input.
