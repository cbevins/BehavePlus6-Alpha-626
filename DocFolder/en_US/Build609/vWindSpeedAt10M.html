10-Meter Wind Speed

10-meter wind speed is the wind 10 meters above the vegetation. The 10-m wind speed is reduced to 20-foot wind speed by dividing by 1.15. For fire spread and intensity calculations in SURFACE, the 20-ft wind speed is further reduced to midflame wind speed according to the wind adjustment factor.

If the SURFACE and SPOT or CROWN modules are active, and if Wind speed entered as midflame height is selected as an input option for SURFACE, there is a conflict because the wind speed required for SPOT or CROWN is at the 20-ft (or 10-m) height. The program offers a choice of how to resolve the conflict.
