Sun-Moon Calendar Location

This page defines the geographic location for the Sun-Moon calendar. The calculations performed by this tool closely match those produced by the Astronomical Applications Department of the U.S. Naval Observatory (to within a minute when the latitude and longitude are specified). The calculations may or may not match those from other sources (e.g., newspapers, commercial websites) because the latitude/longitude that you specify (e.g., site-specific) may be different from that (e.g., airport) used by the source, which may or may not be specified. For large, extended cities or airports that are some distance from the city, calculations can differ by several minutes, depending upon the time of year.

Controls

  • Location
    Enter the location name. The text entered here appears in the title of the sun/moon calendar.
    Press the Browse button to select from over 400,000 named geographic places in the US.

  • GMT Difference
    Select the hours difference from Greenwich Mean Time (GMT). The table below shows the GMT difference for selected time zones; GMT plus the hours from the table yields the local time.

    GMT Time Difference by Time Zone
    Time Zone GMT + h
    GMT Greenwich Mean Time +0
    US Eastern Standard -5
    US Eastern Daylight -4
    US Central Standard -6
    US Central Daylight -5
    US Mountain Standard -7
    US Mountain Daylight -6
    US Pacific Standard -8
    US Pacific Daylight -7
    US Alaska Standard -9
    US Alaska Aleutian Standard -10
    US Hawaiian Standard -10
    Central Europe Summer +2
    Central Europe Standard +1
    Note: If you wish the calendar to display all times in US Daylight Savings Time, you must add one hour to the GMT time difference. For example, for U.S. Mountain Standard Time, GMT difference is -7 hours, so adding 1 hour yields a GMT difference of -6 hours.

  • Latitude/Longitude
    Select the longitude West or East of the Greenwich meridian and latitude North or South of the equator.

  • Degrees
    Enter the location's latitude or longitude in integer degrees (0-180).

  • Minutes
    Enter the location's latitude or longitude whole degree minutes (0-59).

  • Seconds
    Enter the location's latitude or longitude whole degree seconds (0-59).

  • Month
    Select the month for which to calculate sun/moon times.

  • Year
    Enter the year for which to calculate sun/moon times.
