Line Construction Offset

Line construction offset is the distance from the fire at which the suppression line is constructed. Line construction offset distance of zero indicates a direct attack, while any other value indicates a parallel attack.

I/O

Module

If

Notes

Input

CONTAIN

 

  

Output

None

 

 



Direct attack from the rear with line construction offset to zero.
