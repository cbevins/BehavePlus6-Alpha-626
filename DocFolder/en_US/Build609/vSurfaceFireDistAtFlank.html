Flanking Spread Distance

The flanking spread distance is based on the elliptical fire shape model and is the distance the fire travels in a specified period of time in the direction of 90 degrees from the direction of maximum spread.

Module

If

Input None    
Output SIZE   Can also be output as a map distance if Display output distances in map units is checked
