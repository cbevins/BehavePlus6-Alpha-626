Fuel Model Type

Fuel model type is static (S) or dynamic (D) for standard or custom fuel models. Dynamic fuel models have a live herbaceous fuel component, a portion of which is transferred into the dead herbaceous fuel load depending upon the live herbaceous fuel moisture content; the lower the moisture content, the greater the load transfer. The fuel load transfer portion can also be entered directly.

The fuel load transfer equation is:

  • fraction = 1.333 - 0.0111 moisture
where fraction is the fraction of live fuel load transferred from live herbaceous to the dead herbaceous fuel category, and moisture is the live herbaceous fuel moisture content (%). The following table shows some representative values.
