Relative Humidity Tool

Three methods of calculating relative humidity are available.

The first tabbed page (Dry Temp, Wet Temp, & Elev) determines relative humidity, wet bulb depression, and dew point temperature given the dry bulb temperature, wet bulb temperature, and site elevation.

The second tabbed page (Dry Temp, Wet Depress, & Elev) determines relative humidity, wet bulb temperature, and dew point temperature given the dry bulb temperature, wet bulb depression, and site elevation.

The third tabbed page (Dry Temp & Dew Point Temp) determines relative humidity given the dry bulb and dew point temperatures.

The inputs appear at the top of each pane as "spin boxes" displaying up and down arrows. You may enter values directly into the fields using your keypad, or you may click the mouse on the spin box arrows. Click Calculate to calculate the relative humidity and intermediate outputs.

Controls

  • Dry Bulb Temp
    The dry bulb temperature at the site in oF or oC.

  • Wet Bulb Temp
    The wet bulb temperature at the site in oF or oC.

  • Wet Bulb Depression
    The wet bulb temperature depression at the site in oF or oC.

  • Site Elevation
    The site elevation in feet or meters.

  • Dew Point Temp
    The dew point temperature in oF or oC.

  • Relative Humidity
    The relative humidity (%) for the given set of inputs.
