Fireline Constructed

Fireline constructed is the total length of fireline constructed by all resources at the time the fire was contained or determined to have escaped. If the fire is not successfully contained, fireline constructed is the total length of fireline constructed by all resources at the time the last available resource duration expires. If the fire is successfully contained, fireline constructed is the total length of line constructed by all resources at the time of containment.
