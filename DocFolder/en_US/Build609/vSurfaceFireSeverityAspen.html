Aspen Fire Severity

Aspen fire severity is used for predicting mortality of aspen (Brown and Simmerman 1986, Brown and DeByle 1987).

  • Low fire severity in this case refers to fires that char but do not completely consume leaf litter, and have patches of unburned vegetation and litter.
  • Moderate+ severity fires consume litter and some duff.
