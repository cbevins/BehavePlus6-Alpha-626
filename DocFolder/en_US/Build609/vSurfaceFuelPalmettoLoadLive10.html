P-G Live Medium Fuel Load

The P-G live medium fuel load for the palmetto-gallberry fuel model is the load of live palmetto-gallberry stems with 0.25- to 1.0-inch (0.64-2.54 cm) diameters. This variable is calculated from P-G age of rough and P-G height of understory.
