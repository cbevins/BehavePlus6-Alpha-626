Live Fuel Moisture of Extinction

Live fuel moisture of extinction is the characteristic moisture content of the live fuel above which the live fuel is a heat sink. Below that value, the live fuel is a heat source and contributes to the surface fire spread. Live fuel moisture of extinction is calculated from dead fuel moisture, live fuel moisture, load, size, and dead fuel moisture of extinction.
