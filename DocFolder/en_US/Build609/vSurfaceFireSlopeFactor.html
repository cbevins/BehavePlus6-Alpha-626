Slope Factor

The slope factor is an intermediate value in Rothermel's surface fire spread model (1972). Surface rate of spread is calculated for zero wind and zero slope (R0) and then increased by the wind factor (phi-w) and the slope factor (phi-s).

ROS = R0 ( 1 + phi-w + phi-s)
