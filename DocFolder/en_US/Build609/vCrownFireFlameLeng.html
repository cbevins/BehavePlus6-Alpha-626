Crown Flame Length

Crown flame length based on 20-foot wind speeds calculated from crown fireline intensity as described by Rothermel (1991). The flame length equation is from Thomas (1963) and differs from that used for surface fire (Byram 1959). Crown flame length is used to find power of the fire.
