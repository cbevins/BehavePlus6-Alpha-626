Backing Spread Distance

The backing spread distance is based on the elliptical fire shape model and is the distance the fire travels in a specified period of time in the direction of 180 degrees from the direction of maximum spread.

Module

If

Notes
Input None    
Output SIZE   Can also be output as a map distance if Display output distances in map units is checked
