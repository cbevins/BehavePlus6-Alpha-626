100-h Moisture

The 100-h fuel moisture content is the portion of the 100-h timelag dead fuel that is water, calculated on a dry weight basis. The 100-h fuel moisture content has less influence on the fire spread calculations than does the 1-h or 10-h moisture content.

I/O

Module

If

Notes
Input SURFACE If there is 100-h fuel in the fuel model.  
IGNITE If Probability of Ignition from Lightning is selected as output.  
CROWN    
Output None
