Spotting Source Location

The spotting source location refers to the origin of the firebrands that can result in spotting. The possible locations are:

  • Ridge top (RT),
  • Midslope, windward side (MW),
  • Valley bottom (VB), and
  • Midslope, leeward side (ML).
