P-G Dead Fine Fuel Load

The P-G dead fine fuel load for the palmetto-gallberry fuel model is the fuel load of dead palmetto-gallberry stems with 0- to 0.25-inch (0-0.64 cm) diameters. It is calculated from P-G age of rough and P-G height of understory.

Module

Notes
Input None    
Output SURFACE If Fuel is entered as special case fuel model, palmetto-gallberry is selected.
